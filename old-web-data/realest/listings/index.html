Murieta Condo Listings

CURRENT LISTINGS

This is where Murieta homeowners can list their units for sale or rent. If you wish to list your condo, send the info to Murieta Listings. Listings will remain on this page until the unit is sold or pulled off the market. There is no cost to the homeowner to post the listing, but please keep it short and simple!

All condo listings should be in this format (with an illustrative example):

Condo Description: Spacious, lovely 1100 sq. ft paradise overlooking Mission Peak.
Number of Rooms: 2
Number of Bathrooms: 2
Price: $300,000 OBO
Contact Info: Your phone and/or e-mail address

Note: Transactions are strictly made between buyers and sellers, landlords and renters. The Murieta HOA does not endorse, is not liable for, nor profits from these sales or purchases.
