Classified Ads: For Sale

 

Welcome to the Murieta "Goods for Sale" page. This is the core of the Murieta E-Commerce experience: residents having the opportunity to sell their goods online. This is your opportunity to post your items or services you would like advertised. Such good can be household appliances, computers, furniture, autos, office supplies or equipment, tickets, miscellaneous items, or various services.

 

Your ad should include pertinent information about the good or service you have for sale. Here is an illustrative example of what it should contain:

Type of Good or Service: Furniture
Description: 3 year old reclining chair. In good condition.
Cost: $75.00 OBO.
Contact Info: your phone number and/or e-mail address.

 

Once you have made a successful sale, please notify us about the Murieta Goods Sale so we can remove the posting.

Note: Transactions are strictly made between buyers and sellers. The Murieta HOA does not endorse, is not liable for, nor profits from these sales, exchanges, or other types of transactions.
