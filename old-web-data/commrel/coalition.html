Murieta 2001 Coalition:

We are pleased to announce the formation of a coalition to run for the Murieta Board of Directors for the 2001-2 term. We are intent upon making the Murieta a better place to live for all in the community. Our members have a broad range of experiences and ideas that will be beneficial in the coming year.

Here is the platform that we agree will form the basis of our policy making:

* Practice fiscal responsibility
* Commitment to self-management
* Improve property conditions of the common areas
* Proceed with major capital improvements
* Encourage greater community involvement
* Greater responsiveness to homeowner problems & grievances
* Improved relations with vendors, the City of Newark, and neighboring HOAs
* Increased oversite and performance evaluation of Murieta staff
* Continue with the CC&R revision process

We look forward to having the opportunity to serve the community this year.
