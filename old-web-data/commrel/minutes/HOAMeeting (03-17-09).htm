COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Finance Committee Report:

The committee met on February 24 to go over the association’s finances. The March meeting is scheduled for Tuesday, March 24 at 6:30 p.m. in the north clubhouse.

The cash flow budget was presented. Carl Kennedy reviewed Davis-Stirling on borrowing from Reserves and added it may be necessary to borrow the May and June reserve payments; to be repaid in one year.

 

Manager’s Report:

The common area kitchen drain cleaning project was completed in February. The office spoke with the person who leaves dry cat food in the parking lot. This person said she would make arrangements to donate food to the assigned cat feeder. A leak caused by a clogged dryer vent was discovered. Cleaning the dryer vent stopped the intrusion of water. The fountains are showing signs of rust, Ekim Painting is working on a solution to the problem.

 

 

 

Open Forum:  

A resident requested gates on the front driveway. The board discussed police protection. Apparently police drive-through of our parking lots now occurs between 9—10PM rather than the later time of midnight. Another topic discussed was how to educate the community to not block doors open. It was mentioned that the Avalon Apartments at Stevenson and Paseo Padre are completely gated. The board said they would look at that location.
