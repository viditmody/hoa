Presentation by Kim Johansen of Ekim Painting:

 

Mr. Kim Johansen of Ekim Painting Co. presented the advantages of going with a well established, reputable contractor for a large scope project like painting the stucco of all the buildings. He explained the difference between a bid bond (the contractor is financially sound and is capable and well suited to bid on the specific project) and a “performance bond” (it is a guarantee that the results of the project will be as promised in the bid). Mr. Johansen explained the difference between 1 or 2 coats of paint, sheens, quality of materials, warranty and life expectancy.

Ø        Waste Management Update:

Waste management will replace the existing recycling bins and carts. All dumpster enclosures will have either a 2 or 3 cubic feet recycling bin. The goal in increasing the space designated for recyclables is to try to decrease the volume of trash.

 

Murieta will get 3 “bulky item pickups” per year. A large bin will be placed for approx. 4 days in the guest parking spaces facing the back wall (where the car wash used to be). The bins will hold furniture, bicycles and other household goods. Appliances and electronics will need to be placed next to the bin, not inside it. We will announce the dates in the newsletter about a month in advance.

Waste Management accepted Murieta’s under-billing proposed payment plan.. The net amount of $19, 941.64 will be paid in 36 equal monthly installments of $553.93
