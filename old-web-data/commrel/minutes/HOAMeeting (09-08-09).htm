MINUTES

Motion:                Carl Kennedy made a motion to approve the August 25, 2009 HOA meeting minutes as written.

Motion Carried.

 

COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Manager’s Report:

 

Serpico will be our new landscape maintenance contractors starting Nov. 1, 2009.

 

There are ongoing problems in Building 3’s elevator. Otis has come 3 times to troubleshoot the problem.

 

The south side driveways asphalt repair project will take place on Oct. 21 And 22. Six months later, Pacific Surfacing Inc. will be back for the overlay and re-striping of the south side as well as the center driveways and parking lots. 

 

Treeco has recommended the removal of some trees that are dying mostly around the parking lots. The board needs to decide where they want to plant the replacement trees and what species of trees should they be.

The resident that keeps leaving piles of unattended catfood around the south parking lot has continued to do so even after imposing assessments each time it occurs. The Board asked the office to ask the association’s attorney to get involved.

 

 

Open Forum:

 

Resident inquired about whose responsibility it is to repair the glass in her patio door. The seal has been broken and there is moisture  between the 2 glasses. She was advised that the HOA is responsible for such repairs.

 

 

OLD BUSINESS

 

Ø        2010 Reserve study for discussion:

The finance committee needs to study this report and recommend to the board what percentage of funding seems adequate for Murieta.

 

 

NEW BUSINESS:

 

Ø        New loops for automatic gates after asphalt project:

The office will find out exactly how many “loops” will need to be re-installed. The proposal from R&S is not clear. The board will vote on this by e-mail when more information is provided.
