COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Finance Committee:

 

The association’s financial statements look good. We are starting the process of preparing the 2012 operating budget. At the next meeting we should be able to present a more stable budget. All owners are begged to come to the next finance committee meeting on Tuesday, August 23, 2011 at 6:30 p.m. in the north clubhouse.

 

 

Manager’s Report:

 

Antonio, maintenance man, painted the new wood replaced by All Seasons due to dry-rot in the carpots. The cost of painting was $75.

 

The ice cream social event was a huge success. Approximately 80 people attended.

 

The north pool area back wall fence extension is completed.

 

 

Social Committee:

The ice cream and root beer float event was a tremendous success. Most of the people that answered the survey are interested in having a potluck dinner party for the community. The committee also wants to host a morning coffee once a month and alternate venues between the north and the south clubhouses.

 

OPEN FORUM:

 

Security issues were discussed. Car break-ins are still a problem. We will ask R&S if it is possible to cut the time the driveway gates stay open.

All residents need to be vigilant of what goes on around the parking lots. The social events will help for people to get to know their neighbors and then be more prepared when they see someone that “doesn’t belong”.

Ø      Balcony railing coverings:
