COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Manager’s Report:

 

Susan attended de 2010 CACM law seminar. It was extremely interesting at times. There were not many changes to HOA laws during 2009, but a lot of changes will happen this and next year.

 

There was a common area leak in Building 3. The first phase of repairs is done. Murray Construction will complete the work in the next 2 weeks.

 

The fireplace inside the south clubhouse used to burn magazines and books from the shelf.

 

RPM Electric installed new exhaust fans in the shower areas in both clubhouses.

 

 

 

Open Forum:

 

A resident gave her opinion regarding personal liability insurance asking if the association could make it mandatory for all units to purchase such insurance.  The Board answered that even though the HOA has no power to make it mandatory, everyone should have it and we constantly insist on that point in the newsletter.

 

A resident expressed how beneficial closing the perimeter of the complex and installing gates in the center drive way would be.

 

A resident suggested adding more and/or better lighting in the parking lot areas.
