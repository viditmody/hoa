Manager’s Report:

 

In regards to the water leak from a third floor unit in Bldg. 8 that damaged both units underneath it, as well as the common area, we recently found out that the claim was denied. We will ask the HOA’s attorney how to proceed. In the mean time, the amount paid by the HOA has been billed back to the 3rd floor unit where the leak originated.

 

A resident that lives close to the north side swimming pool witnessed a boy climb up the wall to the north pool gate leading to the parking lot. There is a proposal to extend the iron fencing on that area from CWS to be voted on later tonight.

 

Lat month we reported that Newark PD had gained access to Bldg. 6  to check on a resident that reportedly had not been to work in 2 days. They found him unconscious in his bedroom. Paramedics arrived and rushed him to the hospital. We found our recently that the resident very regrettably passed away.

 

The next bulky waste dumpster will be here from August 23 to August 30.

 

Bank of America is working on getting Murieta re-certified for FHA loans. If they are successful the approval will be for the entire Murieta complex. There is no cost to the association for these efforts.

 

 

OPEN FORUM:

 

Resident informed that there are some bad re-appearing stains on the Building 5, 3rd floor hallway carpet. Mr. Unlimited will be here this week to steam clean that building.
