Ø        All Seasons Roofing representative discussing roof maintenance:

Brad Meyer from All Seasons explained the following to the Board…

The roofs were new in 2001. The 5 year maintenance was performed in 2006.

The roof system requires semi-annual maintenance because there are lots of penetrations (chimneys, sky lights, metal caps, vents, flashing, solar panels, etc.). All that sealant material (caulk, plastic cement, etc.) is good for a maximum of 18 months. He proposes a major maintenance appointment annually to inspect, maintain, repair and reseal all penetrations on and around the roofs. This service appointment would also include general cleaning of debris. A “minor maintenance service appointment” would be needed 6 months later for general cleaning of debris.

Every 5 years all roofs will need to be completely re-sealed.

He will forward a proposal to the office.
