COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Manager’s Report:

There is a unit that has been foreclosed by the Association for unpaid dues. The owner has 90 days to redeem his debt. The Board asked the office to ask our attorney what would happen if the HOA becomes the owner of record.

 

There are two samples of clear “lollipop” lights behind Building # 3. Office asked the Board and residents present to please take a look at them and compare the light output compared to the existing ones. This item will be included in next month’s meeting agenda.

 

We have new signs for the driveway gates, but will not install them until the gates are ready to become operational.

 

The community yard sale held last month was very successful. 19 residents registered to participate as vendors.

 

The office has distributed  gates access cards to 234 units so far. We still have 90 left. We are waiting for the final city inspection before the new center driveway gates become operational.

 

Finance Committee Report:

 

The finance committee met on August 31 to review the financial statements. The amount of accounts and money that are now in collections is of great concern. The process of preparing the 2011 budget has begun and we are desperately trying to implement ways to cut operational expenses and try to manage the cash flow budget.

Next meeting will be Tuesday, September 21, 2010 at 6:30 p.m. in the north clubhouse.

 

Open Forum:

 

A resident mentioned that there are some patios and balconies with “junk” in them.  Only outdoor furniture is allowed on patios and balconies.

 

A resident complained about noise late at night at the pool. He mentioned a resident yelling from a bullhorn asking people to leave the pool area, but that the bullhorn is even more disruptive than the people at the pool.
