MINUTES

Motion:                        Joy Hurst made a motion to approve October 11, 2005 HOA minutes.

2nd:                              Karen Barry

 

Vote:                           Approved: May McMann, Carl Kennedy, Karen Barry, Joy Hurst

                                               

Motion Carried.

 

OPEN FORUM

 

Homeowner asked if Murieta had ever hired a structural engineer to provide a seismic evaluation of the buildings. Answer: No. Office to research this question.

 

Homeowner asked what to do about a neighboring stereo played at high volume. Answer: File a CC&R violation complaint or call the police.

 

Homeowner inquired about adding a handicapped access ramp to the front of a building. Answer: structural change would require a vote of 51% of the homeowners. Office to research adding two more sidewalk ramps to the parking lots.

 

COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

It was decided to find a local charity to provide barrels for donations of non-perishable food items and toys this holiday season. Office to contact Newark LOV.

wash area into two guest parking places.

Ø       Review of Clubhouse rental rules

It was decided to change clubhouse rental hours to: Sunday – Thursday parties must be out of the clubhouse by 7 PM and clean-up finished by 8 PM. Friday and Saturday parties must be out by 9 PM and clean-up finished by 10 PM.

 

Any outside items or entertainment brought in for the purpose of the party must have prior approval from the Board of Directors.
