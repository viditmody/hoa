Ø        Report on CC&R Update Process:

There is a new whole set of laws that will come into effect on July 1st, 2006 regulating Home Owner Associations. These new laws make our current CC&Rs and Bylaws obsolete.  Attorneys are in the process of updating them and bringing them into compliance with the new laws. Newly revised documents will be submitted to all homeowners for a vote late this summer.
