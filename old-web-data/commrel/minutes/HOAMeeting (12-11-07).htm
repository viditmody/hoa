OPEN FORUM:

 

Resident was inquiring about security in the parking lots.

 

Resident was inquiring about cobwebs on exterior of buildings. The Board asked the office to research the possibility of a long reach brush to clean them off.

 

Resident was commenting that his neighbors below smoke on the patio and the smoke travels into his unit. He was wondering if there is anything the Association could do to prevent the problems from 2nd hand smoke.

 

 

COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

 

Office Report:

 

RPM electric re-lighted all chandeliers.

 

All eight elevators have the new ceilings installed.

 

The Salvation Army “used clothes barrel” was a success. It turned into 4 full barrels.

 

There were several extinguishers’ breaker bars stolen. Some were replaced and immediately stolen again.

 

Waste Management increased its rate by 32%. The notice of this increase and possible further increases came after the budget for 2008 was finalized.

 

The Fire Alarm System inspection has been scheduled for the week of Dec 17 to Dec. 21, 2007.  Signs were posted on Nov. 28 on all exterior doors. Letters were sent to all off-site owners, the schedule was announced in the December newsletter and a 2nd reminder was mailed to all residents this week.

 

It was reported to the office that someone is jamming the door to the back parking lot in building 2 in a way that prevents it from locking. Also in building 2, there is a small dog walking around off-leashed.

The Board asked the office to post signs in building 2 reminding residents not to meddle with the doors and that all pets must be on a leash (per Murieta rules and Newark ordinance). The office will contact Store Front Doors to ask if there is any way to make it more difficult to disable the locking mechanism on doors.

 

 

Finance Committee Report:

 

The committee met on November 20, to go over the association’s finances.

The December meeting is scheduled for Tuesday, December 18 at 6:30 p.m. in the north clubhouse.
