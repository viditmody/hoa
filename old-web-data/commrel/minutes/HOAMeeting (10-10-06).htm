COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

 

 

Office Report:

 

Ernest Saffings (maintenance man) was out for two weeks due to illness. During his absence, Ecoloclean took over a few of his duties. They were very accommodating and gracious about the extra tasks.

 

The gym’s treadmill’s console was removed and is being repaired by Club Care. The treadmill should be back in service in a week or two.

 

The defibrillator inside the gym was making a beeping sound. Statcomm disarmed the alarm so the Newark Fire Department would not be called when the case was opened. It seems to be a battery needing replacement.

 

The P.G.&E. bill for the south clubhouse went up $200 compared to the previous month. Sparkle Pool and Spa identified a spa jet timer being off. We are still researching what else could be the cause of this hike.

 

Lewis Carpet Cleaners is coming next week to clean the common area carpets in Building No. 3.  Ecoloclean removed most stains in Building 6.

 

Finance Committee Report:

 

Last meeting was held on Tuesday, September 26. Topics addressed were the general finances and the 2007 budget.

 

2006 turned out to be a difficult year because of the reserve funding and because we were hit with quite significant water damage. There still might be a few windows that will need to be replaced.

 

On Tuesday, October 24, 2006 there will be a Finance Committee meeting directly followed by a special Board meeting.

 

Feral Cat Update:

 

2 cats were caught, neutered and re-released.

 Foster homes were found for 2 of the kittens.

The association bought a second trap.

A resident volunteered to try to trap 3 kittens and look for adoption options
