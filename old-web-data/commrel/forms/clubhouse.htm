CLUBHOUSE USAGE AGREEMENT

 

TERMS AND CONDITIONS

 

 

1.                  A rental fee of $50.00 will be paid for use of the clubhouse and janitorial services to clean bathrooms and floors. This fee pertains to general janitorial services that require a reasonable time—one (1) hour--to complete. Any additional time necessary to clean the facilities beyond this reasonable time of one (1) hour will cause additional amounts to be paid by the homeowner.

2.                  Owners are responsible for the actions of their guests and shall hold the Association harmless from and defend the Association against any and all claims or liability for any injury, damage to any person or property whatsoever when such injury or damage shall be caused in whole or in part by the act, neglect or fault of the homeowner, his/her tenants, agents, servants, employees or invitees.

3.                  No alcoholic beverages are to be served or consumed on the premises.

4.                  All parties must be out by 7 P.M. Sunday—Thursday with clean-up completed by 8 P.M.; Friday and Saturday all parties must be out by 9 P.M. and clean-up completed by 10 P.M. Fee of $100 per hour thereafter.

5.                  Key must be returned the following business day or owner will be charged the going daily rental rate until key is returned.

6.                  An assessment will be levied against the owner for any damage to the clubhouse or surrounding areas. CC&R violations will be assessed as follows: Littering: $100. Parking violation: $200/vehicle. Gates/doors propped open: $100/gate or door.
