COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Finance Committee Report:

 

The finance committee met earlier tonight to review the financial statements. On top of the cuts already implemented and/or approved, we still need to find $3,000-$5,000 in savings in the next quarter to cut operational expenses and try to manage the cash flow budget.

Next meeting will be Tuesday, November 23, 2010 at 6:30 p.m. in the north clubhouse.

 

Manager’s Report:

 

Per the recommendations of a majority of the Board, this year we will not be providing “Trick or Treaters welcome/not welcome” Halloween flyers.

The pool furniture will be removed this week before it starts raining.

 

There have been 2 vehicle break-ins reported to the office since the center driveway gates became operational.

 

There is a resident on back of Building 6 that has installed flood lights on a tripod in his patio to provide more light directed at his parking space. Manager asked the Board to please take a look at them and determine if it is OK to leave them there.

 

We had a representative from Zenith Insurance come to the office to conduct a workman’s comp insurance audit.

 

Maintenance man took over the cleaning of the dumpster areas and taking the dumpsters in and out on trash days. This week he is working on cleaning the common area dryer vent covers.

 

The annual fire alarm testing and inspection is completed. We are still waiting on Statcomm’s report of number of no shows and failed devices.

 

The rental waiting list is moving along. We have 2 more units, which are currently rentals, on the market.

 

We have kept a log of residents’ complaints and/or suggestions regarding the center driveway gates. One of the most recurring ones is that the time it takes the gates to close again after a car has come in is too long. The Board asked the office to shorten that time to 10 seconds.
