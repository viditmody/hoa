Ø        Donation Barrel for Old Clothes:

The Board is concerned about the potential for abuse a permanent “donation sight” inside Murieta could bring.

The office is to contact charity organizations to see if they would be willing to bring a barrel for a couple of days quarterly or semi annually.

The community should be educated in regards to suitable places to recycle electronics, etc.
