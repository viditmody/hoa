Finance Committee Report:

 

The June meeting took place on June 20.

 

Marilyn Bell is working on a cash flow projection which should be finished in the next few days.

Next meeting will take place on Tuesday July 18, 2006 at 6:30 pm at the north club house.

The north backflow device has been replaced and repairs on the south backflow have been scheduled for Tuesday, July 18, 2006.

“Club Care” fixed all machines inside the weight room. There are a couple of pads that have been backordered.

 

The cover for the south side Spa is broken. The spa will be closed until the necessary part arrives and is installed.
