COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Finance Committee Report:

The committee met on August 18 to review the association’s financial statements.

The committee worked on the third draft of the 2010 budget. Some items still need clarification but hopefully, they will have fairly solid numbers for the budget at the next meeting.

Next meeting will be on Tuesday, September 22, 2009.

 

 

Manager’s Report:

 

Janitors have cleaned urine from the Building 6 elevator 4 days in a row. We posted a sign offering a small reward for any information.

 

Another resident has requested to include an ad for math tutoring in the newsletter.  We explained that it will run space permitting.

 

The Salvation Army donation drive was yet again, an overwhelming success. The 5 barrels were overflowing with clothes donations.

 

The bulky item dumpster was full to capacity when Waste Management came to retrieve it. The only problem is that we found a couple of items left by residents after the dumpster had gone.

 

There is a common area leak in Building 4. Conway Plumbing is making all necessary repairs.

 

National Night Out was not very well attended, but thanks to all the ice cream and root beers left over, we are having root beer floats and ice cream sundaes for all attendees at this meeting.

 

Open Forum:

 

Resident expressed concern about planters dripping water and potentially rotting the wood on patios and balconies. A board member reminded her that as long as water drips on painted wood, it should not be a problem.

 

Resident inquired about security cameras out in parking lots and driveways to try to deter car break-ins. The Board asked the office to try to create a list of the different options available for security cameras.

 

Resident reported that the elevator in Building 3 gets stuck on the 2nd and 3rd floor from time to time. The first floor button is apparently not working 100%.  The office will call Otis for service.
