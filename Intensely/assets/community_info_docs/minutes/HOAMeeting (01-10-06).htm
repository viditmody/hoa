COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Common Area Manager Susan Levin reported:

 

There is still a “trouble” signal in Bldg 1, that creates beeping in Bldg 5. Statcomm will be trouble shooting this on Wednesday, Jan 17th, 2006.

 

All Seasons Roofing responded quickly to a leak call on January 05, 2006. There is a slight chance some of the material used in the re-roofing project in 2001 (particular  nails) are faulty. All Seasons will send a crew out to retrofit all areas involved – at their cost.

 

Murray Construction is working on repairs of common area leaks in Bldg. 3 & 4. They will order the new window for Bldg. 5 which will probably take about three weeks.

 

There is evidence of drug abuse on Murieta property.

The improvements in the property last year:

  • New common area carpets.
  • Walk-off mats to protect the carpets.
  • Additional time/money for clean-up of red tile and dumpster areas.
  • Termite protection in the form of the re-built meter closets, repair to termite damaged fences, repair of damaged flashing.
  • New patio furniture.
  • Electronic entry system for the recreation areas.
  • Handicap accessible ramps.
