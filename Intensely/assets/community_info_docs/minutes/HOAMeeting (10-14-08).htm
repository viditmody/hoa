COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Finance Committee Report:

The committee met on September 23 to go over the association’s finances. The committee prepared the final draft of the 2009 operating budget for the Board’s approval this evening. The October meeting is scheduled for Tuesday, October 21 at 6:30 p.m. in the north clubhouse.

 

Manager’s Report:

 

The re-finishing of the north spa with a fiberglass coating has not started. The environmental & health department needs to come and perform an inspection. Allen’s Pool Co. believes they will be able to start in about two weeks.

 

The two trees that caused damage on a section of the back wall have been removed. Murray Construction is waiting on permits from the City of Newark to start the wall replacement project.

Golden Bear Extinguisher Co. came and replaced 11 breaker bars throughout the complex.

 

Bell Plumbing will be here on the last week of October to perform the bi-annual north side common area kitchen drain hydro flushing. We are working on securing appointment times for all first floor units.

 

Club Care is scheduled to come later this week to repair the elliptical machine and to change some missing cables in the multi-purpose machine.

 

We are still getting frequent calls from Bay Alarm (10 this week)  for “trouble on zone 4”.
