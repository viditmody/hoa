Ø        Compliance with new HOA voting laws:

Murieta office can act as the inspector of election. Owners don’t necessarily have to mail the ballot; it can be dropped in a locked box at the office.

The new rules provided by Michael Hardy (attorney) would permit the association to comply with the laws that will come into effect July 1, 2006.

Before the Association can adopt these rules, they will be sent to all members for review and comments. After 30 days, the board will vote on the new rules.

Murieta’s existing CC&R’s and Bylaws do not comply with the new state laws.
