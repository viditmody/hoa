COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Manager’s Report:

We haven’t had graffiti in the last month.

 

Clubcare took a look at the elliptical machine in the gym and said that this particular model suffers from a design flaw.

2 bicycles were reported stolen in one week.

A resident spoke against the potential rule change regarding the pool hours. He would like it to remain open until 10:30 p.m. and not change to 10:00 p.m.

 

Another resident spoke in favor of the pool hours change.

 

A resident complained about the increasing number of shopping carts left in the hallways by other residents.
