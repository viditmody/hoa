Tim Young called the meeting to order at 7:05 P.M.

 

Victor Guerra from Air Doctor gave a presentation regarding dryer vent cleaning: he explained how  a clogged vent can  ignite and start a fire or cause water damage when vapor cannot escape and condenses. He spoke about the best practices to properly clean a dryer vent and how often it should be done.   A summary of the presentation will be posted on the Murieta website.

COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Manager’s Report:

 

Charissa Gant and Susan Levin bought new furniture for the south clubhouse per the passed motion last month. They were able to find a good deal and stayed within budget. The new furniture will be delivered this Thursday.

 

There was a leak from a hot water line in building 6. Both units affected had insurance and the insurance companies stepped up and performed as expected.  We have asked the residents involved to write an article for the newsletter. We can tell the community of the importance of having insurance but maybe the message will get through if fellow residents give their testimonials.

In two other units there were common area  plumbing repairs that amounted to over $4,000. Question: Should these be considered an operational expense or a reserve item?

 

PG&E has informed us that this Thursday (April 12, 2012) they will have to interrupt service on the following schedule. Buildings 5 and 6 will have no power from 7:00 a.m. until 3:00 p.m. All other buildings will have a brief disruption of service for about 20 minutes between 7 and 8 a.m. and again between 2 and 3 p.m.  PG&E sent individual letters to all residents to inform them. Maintenance manager, Antonio Duran, will come very early that day to post signs on elevators and building front doors. Since the power to the center driveway gates comes from building 5, they will be opened and remain open for the duration of the power outage. Per the Board, the front doors to Buildings 5 and 6 are to remain closed with a sign advising residents to use side doors will be posted.

 

The bulky waste bin will be delivered Tuesday, April 10 and picked up Tuesday, the 17th.

 

Open Forum:

 

A resident inquired about the social committee and when it would plan  get-togethers and events. The possibility of re-starting in May was discussed. Maybe if residents can just come, and not be asked to bring food, more people would show up.

 

A resident, who recently was involved in a plumbing leak incident, asked if there were emergency numbers to contact on weekends. He wanted to ask someone how to shut the water off.  We will include instructions on how to shut the water off, in case of emergencies, in the next newsletter and we will post the information on our website.

 

Annual Election Results:

The members of the Board of Directors for the 2012-2013 term are Charissa Gant, Tim Young, Joy Hurst, Carl Kennedy and Raj Kewal. Margot Elliot is willing to step up in case a board member moves or can no longer serve.

The IRS resolution passed.

We needed 163 votes total to entertain the question about earthquake insurance. We only got 115 ballots back. Therefore, Murieta will not be buying earthquake insurance.
