COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Finance Committee:

The committee met on February 21 to go over the association’s finances. Recommendations from the meeting are listed under New Business.

 

Manager’s Report:

 

Manager responded to question from last month about adding access devices to the outlying parking lot driveway gates. Quote for programmable radio receivers on both gates was included in original estimate from R&S. Total cost would be $7,120. Additionally, each location would require installation of power and a phone line. Access would then be available to all clickers; this system would not work with the white cards.

 

An inebriated man entered Bldg. 5 about 6:30PM on Wednesday, Feb. 29. He pounded on unit doors, trying to gain access. The children of at least one family were badly frightened. Two residents called Newark PD. They responded and took the drunk into custody.  Board suggested that walk-off mat should be moved back away from the front door of Bldg. 5.

 

Carpets were dry cleaned in Bldg. 1, 2 and 3 this week.

 

Manager described a frightening incident involving a resident, maintenance manager and the police which occurred last week.

 

Open forum:

Resident asked about requiring that all dryer vents be cleaned. Office told to discuss with Air Doctor.
