COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Manager’s Report:

 

The janitors are cleaning cat food from uncovered parking space # 133 almost everyday.

 

The traps set up by Critter control caught a large female raccoon inside the meter closet of Bldg. 6.

 

The Salvation Army donation bin has been very successful.

 

Murray Construction installed a rodent screen inside the meter closet of Building 6.

 

We have reason to believe that there is a unit in building 1 that is being rented “illegally”.

 

Our Otis elevator technician shared with us that even though the upgrades proposed would be “nice to have” they are completely optional and that the elevators are in good working order.

 

 

Finance Committee Report:

 

The committee met earlier tonight. All financials are in order. Next meeting will be on February 16, 2010 at 6:30 p.m.
