Board of Directors Meeting

Meeting was called to order by President May McMann at 7:05 PM. Board members are:

May McMann President Present

Joy Hurst Vice President Absent

Carl Kennedy Treasurer Present

Karen Barry Secretary Present

Kristen Brown Director Present

Also in attendance: Susan Levin and Liza Laurinkus.

 

 

MINUTES

Motion: Karen Barry made a motion to approve July 12, 2005 HOA minutes.

2nd: Kristen Brown

Motion Vote: Approved: May McMann, Carl Kennedy, Karen Barry, Kristen Brown.

Motion Carried.

OPEN FORUM

The issues of new carpet spots treating and outside red tile cleaning were discussed.

COMMITTEE REPORTS

Common area manager Susan Levin told that the door mats should be in place on Wednesday, Aug. 17, 2005. False alarm in Bldg 1 is still causing system problems, but Statcomm wasn't able to locate the source.

OLD BUSINESS

New Member orientation meeting

The Board recommended buying 5 copies of Condominium Bluebook to keep on the shelves at the N. Clubhouse.

The Board members will review the presentation material and will submit their suggestions.

Pizza and soft drinks will be served at the orientation meeting.

 

 

 

 

NEW BUSINESS

  • Statcomm Fire Alarm System Service Proposal

Motion: Carl Kennedy made a motion to accept Statcomm's proposal in the amount of $9,720.00 for fire alarm system testing and inspection starting with Bldg 1.

Second: Kristen Brown.

Vote: All in favor: Carl Kennedy, May McMann, Kristen Brown, Karen Barry.

Motion carried.
  • Pacheco Proposal for Parking Lot maintenance.

Motion: Carl Kennedy made a motion to accept Pacheco Brothers' proposal in the amount of $1,500.00 per month for blowing parking lots & carports 1x per week, hauling debris.

Second: Kristen Brown.

Vote: All in favor: Carl Kennedy, May McMann, Kristen Brown, Karen Barry.

Motion carried.

  • New Signs for HOA Meeting Notification.

Signs Now Fremont proposal in the amount of $376.67 was approved by the President of HOA Board of Directors May McMann.
  • Carport Roof Repair.

Motion: Carl Kennedy made a motion to accept All Seasons Roofing Services' proposal in the amount of $1,180.00 to replace missing 7 destroyed tiles.

Second: Karen Barry.

Vote: All in favor: Carl Kennedy, May McMann, Kristen Brown, Karen Barry.

Motion carried.

 

  • Paint French Doors, North Clubhouse

Vote: All in favor: Carl Kennedy, May McMann, Kristen Brown, Karen Barry.

Motion carried.

  • Far East National Bank CD Matures 08/12/05.
