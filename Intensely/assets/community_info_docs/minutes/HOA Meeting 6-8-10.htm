Results of second election on additional gates in parking lots:

There were 92 votes in favor of installing gates in the center parking lot and 83 votes against it.  The next step will be to coordinate the details and contact the vendor to schedule installation.

COMMITTEE REPORTS

 

Manager’s Report:

Maintenance man observed how Air Doctor performed the semi-annual common area dryer vent cleaning on the roofs. It is very simple and he is confident he can do it next time and save the HOA money.

Maintenance man changed a faulty doorknob in the men’s room in the south clubhouse. We would normally call a locksmith for this.

 

The solar heaters for the pools have been turned on. We will need about a month of warm weather to really feel the difference in the water’s temperature.

 

All fire extinguishers were serviced and inspected last month.

 

Maintenance man, Antonio, washed all the skylights. A lot more light is coming into the 3rd floor hallways.

 

We have a new suspected unauthorized rental.

 

There is an AT&T hatch outside Building 4 that is in very bad condition and is a serious safety hazard. AT&T placed cones and “caution” tape around it. They will install a more permanent solution but it will take a couple of months.
